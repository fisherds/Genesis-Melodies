Genesis 14

Chapter 14

And it came about in the days of Amraphel, king of Shinar, of Aryok, king of Elasar, of Kedorlaomer, king of Elam, and of Tidal, king of Goyyim,
1And it came to pass in the days of Amraphel king of Shinar Arioch king of Ellasar Chedorlaomer king of Elam and Tidal king of nations
וַיְהִ֗י בִּימֵי֙ אַמְרָפֶ֣ל מֶ֥לֶךְ שִׁנְעָ֔ר אַרְי֖וֹךְ מֶ֥לֶךְ אֶלָּסָ֑ר כְּדָרְלָעֹ֙מֶר֙ מֶ֥לֶךְ עֵילָ֔ם וְתִדְעָ֖ל מֶ֥לֶךְ גּוֹיִֽם׃
that they made war with Bera’, king of Sodom, and with Birsha’, king of Gomorrah, and with Shinab, king of ‘Admah, and with Shemever, king of Tsiboyim, and with the king of Bela [this is Zoar].
2That these made war with Bera and the king of Sodom with and with Birsha and the king of Gomorrah Shinab and the king of Admah and Shemeber and the king of Zeboiim and the king of Bela which is Zoar
עָשׂ֣וּ מִלְחָמָ֗ה וְאֶת בֶּ֙רַע֙ וּמֶ֥לֶךְ סְדֹ֔ם וְאֶת בִּרְשַׁ֖ע וּמֶ֥לֶךְ עֲמֹרָ֑ה שִׁנְאָ֣ב׀ וּמֶ֥לֶךְ אַדְמָ֗ה וְשֶׁמְאֵ֙בֶר֙ וּמֶ֥לֶךְ צְבֹיִ֔ים וּמֶ֥לֶךְ בֶּ֖לַע הִיא צֹֽעַר׃
All of these joined together in the valley of Siddim [this is the Sea of Salt].
3All these were joined together in the vale of Siddim which sea is the salt
כָּל אֵ֙לֶּה֙ חָֽבְר֔וּ אֶל עֵ֖מֶק הַשִּׂדִּ֑ים ה֖וּא יָ֥ם הַמֶּֽלַח׃
For 12 years they had been slaves of Kedorlaomer, and in the 13th year they rebelled,
4Twelve year they served Chedorlaomer and in the thirteenth Twelve year they rebelled
שְׁתֵּ֤ים עֶשְׂרֵ֥ה שָׁנָ֖ה עָֽבְד֖וּ אֶת כְּדָרְלָעֹ֑מֶר וּשְׁלֹשׁ עֶשְׂרֵ֥ה שָׁנָ֖ה מָרָֽדוּ׃
and in the 14th year Kedorlaomer came, and the kings who were with him, and they struck the Rephaim in Ashteroth-Qarnayim (= Asherah of Twin-Peaks) and the Zuzim in Ham, and the ‘Emim in Shaveh-Qiryathayim (the Plain of Twin-Cities),
5And in the fourteenth year came Chedorlaomer and the kings that were with him and smote the Rephaims in Ashteroth Karnaim and the Zuzims in Ham and the Emims in Shaveh Kiriathaim
וּבְאַרְבַּע֩ עֶשְׂרֵ֨ה שָׁנָ֜ה בָּ֣א כְדָרְלָעֹ֗מֶר וְהַמְּלָכִים֙ אֲשֶׁ֣ר אִתּ֔וֹ וַיַּכּ֤וּ אֶת רְפָאִים֙ בְּעַשְׁתְּרֹ֣ת קַרְנַ֔יִם וְאֶת הַזּוּזִ֖ים בְּהָ֑ם וְאֵת֙ הָֽאֵימִ֔ים בְּשָׁוֵ֖ה קִרְיָתָֽיִם׃
and the Horites in the mountains of Se’ir, unto the Oak of Paran which is by the wilderness.
6And the Horites in their mount Seir unto Elparan which is by the wilderness
וְאֶת הַֽחֹרִ֖י בְּהַרְרָ֣ם שֵׂעִ֑יר עַ֚ד אֵ֣יל פָּארָ֔ן אֲשֶׁ֖ר עַל הַמִּדְבָּֽר׃
And they returned and they went to Eyn-Mishpat (the Spring of Judgment) [this is Qadesh] and they struck all the field of the Amalekites, and also the Amorites who dwell in Hatzetzon-Tamar (Heap of Palms)
7And they returned and came to Enmishpat which is Kadesh and smote all the country of the Amalekites and also the Amorites that dwelt in Hazezontamar
וַ֠יָּשֻׁבוּ וַיָּבֹ֜אוּ אֶל עֵ֤ין מִשְׁפָּט֙ הִ֣וא קָדֵ֔שׁ וַיַּכּ֕וּ אֶֽת כָּל שְׂדֵ֖ה הָעֲמָֽלֵקִ֑י וְגַם֙ אֶת הָ֣אֱמֹרִ֔י הַיֹּשֵׁ֖ב בְּחַֽצְצֹ֥ן תָּמָֽר׃
And the king of Sodom went out, and the king of Gomorrah, and the king of ‘Admah, and the king of Tsiboyim, and the king of Bela [this is Zoar] and they lined themselves up for war in the valley of Siddim,
8And there went out and the king of Sodom and the king of Gomorrah and the king of Admah and the king of Zeboiim and the king of Bela the same is Zoar and they joined battle with them in the vale of Siddim
וַיֵּצֵ֨א וּמֶ֥לֶךְ סְדֹ֜ם וּמֶ֥לֶךְ עֲמֹרָ֗ה וּמֶ֥לֶךְ אַדְמָה֙ וּמֶ֥לֶךְ צְבֹיִ֔ים וּמֶ֥לֶךְ בֶּ֖לַע הִוא צֹ֑עַר וַיַּֽעַרְכ֤וּ אִתָּם֙ מִלְחָמָ֔ה בְּעֵ֖מֶק הַשִּׂדִּֽים׃
with Kedorlaomer, king of Elam, and Tidal, king of Goyyim and Amraphel, king of Shinar, and Aryok, king of Elasar, four kings against five.
9With Chedorlaomer king of Elam and with Tidal king of nations and Amraphel king of Shinar and Arioch king of Ellasar four king with five
אֵ֣ת כְּדָרְלָעֹ֜מֶר מְלָכִ֖ים עֵילָ֗ם וְתִדְעָל֙ מְלָכִ֖ים גּוֹיִ֔ם וְאַמְרָפֶל֙ מְלָכִ֖ים שִׁנְעָ֔ר וְאַרְי֖וֹךְ מְלָכִ֖ים אֶלָּסָ֑ר אַרְבָּעָ֥ה מְלָכִ֖ים אֶת הַֽחֲמִשָּֽׁה׃
Now, the valley of Siddim was full of many pits of tar, and they fled, the king of Sodom, and Gomorrah, and they fell into there, and those remaining fled to the mountains.
10And the vale of Siddim slimepits slimepits fled and the kings of Sodom and Gomorrah and fell there and they that remained to the mountain fled
וְעֵ֣מֶק הַשִׂדִּ֗ים בֶּֽאֱרֹת֙ בֶּֽאֱרֹת֙ חֵמָ֔ר נָּֽסוּ׃ מֶֽלֶךְ סְדֹ֥ם וַֽעֲמֹרָ֖ה וַיִּפְּלוּ שָׁ֑מָּה וְהַנִּשְׁאָרִ֖ים הֶ֥רָה נָּֽסוּ׃
And they took all the possessions of Sodom and Gomorrah, and all their food, and they went away,
11And they took all the goods of Sodom and Gomorrah and all their victuals
וַ֠יִּקְחוּ אֶת כָּל רְכֻ֨שׁ סְדֹ֧ם וַֽעֲמֹרָ֛ה וְאֶת כָּל אָכְלָ֖ם וַיֵּלֵֽכוּ׃
and they took Lot and his possessions, the son of the brother of Avram, and they went away.
12And they took Lot and his goods son brother's Abram's who dwelt in Sodom
וַיִּקְח֨וּ אֶת ל֧וֹט וְאֶת רְכֻשׁ֛וֹ בֶּן אֲחִ֥י אַבְרָ֖ם וַיֵּלֵ֑כוּ וְה֥וּא יֹשֵׁ֖ב בִּסְדֹֽם׃
Now, he had been dwelling in Sodom. And a refugee came and reported to Avram the Hebrew. Now, he was residing among the oaks of Mamre the Amorite, the brother of Eshkol, and the brother of Aner; now, they were members of a covenant with Avram.
13And there came one that had escaped and told with Abram the Hebrew for he dwelt in the plain of Mamre the Amorite and brother of Eshcol and brother of Aner and these were confederate with Abram
וַיָּבֹא֙ הַפָּלִ֔יט וַיַּגֵּ֖ד אַבְרָֽם׃ הָֽעִבְרִ֑י וְהוּא֩ שֹׁכֵ֨ן בְּאֵֽלֹנֵ֜י מַמְרֵ֣א הָֽאֱמֹרִ֗י וַֽאֲחִ֣י אֶשְׁכֹּל֙ וַֽאֲחִ֣י עָנֵ֔ר וְהֵ֖ם בַּֽעֲלֵ֥י בְרִית אַבְרָֽם׃
And Avram heard that his brother had been taken captive, and he brought out his trained men, those born of his house, 318, and he chased unto Dan.
14heard And when Abram was taken captive that his brother he armed his trained servants born in his own house and eighteen three hundred and pursued them unto Dan
וַיִּשְׁמַ֣ע אַבְרָ֔ם כִּ֥י נִשְׁבָּ֖ה אָחִ֑יו וַיָּ֨רֶק אֶת חֲנִיכָ֜יו יְלִידֵ֣י בֵית֗וֹ שְׁמֹנָ֤ה עָשָׂר֙ וּשְׁלֹ֣שׁ מֵא֔וֹת וַיִּרְדֹּ֖ף עַד דָּֽן׃
And he divided up against them by night, he and his servants, and he struck them, and he chased them unto Khobah, which is north of Damascus,
15And he divided by night himself against them he and his servants and smote them and pursued them unto Hobah which is on the left hand of Damascus
וַיֵּֽחָלֵ֨ק עֲלֵיהֶ֧ם׀ לַ֛יְלָה ה֥וּא וַֽעֲבָדָ֖יו וַיַּכֵּ֑ם וַֽיִּרְדְּפֵם֙ עַד חוֹבָ֔ה אֲשֶׁ֥ר מִשְּׂמֹ֖אל לְדַמָּֽשֶׂק׃
and he returned all the possessions, and also Lot his brother and his possessions he returned, and also the women and the people.
16And he brought back all the goods Lot his brother all the goods And he brought back and the women also and the people
הֵשִׁ֔יב אֵ֖ת כָּל וּרְכֻשׁוֹ֙ וְגַם֩ אֶת ל֨וֹט אָחִ֤יו וּרְכֻשׁוֹ֙ הֵשִׁ֔יב וְגַ֥ם אֶת הַנָּשִׁ֖ים וְאֶת הָעָֽם׃
And the king of Sodom went out to meet him, after he returned from striking Kedorla’omer, and the kings with him, into the valley of the plain [this is the valley of the king].
17went out And the king of Sodom to meet him after his return from the slaughter of Chedorlaomer And the king dale of Shaveh which dale And the king
וַיֵּצֵ֣א הַמֶּֽלֶךְ׃ סְדֹם֮ לִקְרָאתוֹ֒ אַֽחֲרֵ֣י שׁוּב֗וֹ מֵֽהַכּוֹת֙ אֶת כְּדָרְלָעֹ֔מֶר וְאֶת הַמֶּֽלֶךְ׃ אֲשֶׁ֣ר אִתּ֑וֹ אֶל עֵ֥מֶק שָׁוֵ֔ה ה֖וּא עֵ֥מֶק הַמֶּֽלֶךְ׃
And Melki-zedeq, king of Shalem, brought out bread and wine.
18And Melchizedek king of Salem brought forth bread and wine and he was the priest God of the most high
וּמַלְכִּי צֶ֙דֶק֙ מֶ֣לֶךְ שָׁלֵ֔ם הוֹצִ֖יא לֶ֣חֶם וָיָ֑יִן וְה֥וּא כֹהֵ֖ן לְאֵ֥ל עֶלְיֽוֹן׃
Now, he was the priest of El-Most High. And he blessed him, and he said: “May Avram be blessed by El-Most High, the Possessor of skies and land.
19And he blessed him and said And he blessed be Abram God of the most high possessor of heaven and earth
בָּר֤וּךְ וַיֹּאמַ֑ר בָּר֤וּךְ אַבְרָם֙ לְאֵ֣ל עֶלְי֔וֹן קֹנֵ֖ה שָׁמַ֥יִם וָאָֽרֶץ׃
And may El-Most High be blessed, who has delivered your adversaries into your hand.” And he gave him a tenth of everything.
20And blessed God be the most high which hath delivered thine enemies into thy hand And he gave him tithes
וּבָרוּךְ֙ אֵ֣ל עֶלְי֔וֹן אֲשֶׁר מִגֵּ֥ן צָרֶ֖יךָ בְּיָדֶ֑ךָ וַיִּתֶּן ל֥וֹ מַֽעֲשֵׂ֖ר מִכֹּֽל׃
And the king of Sodom said to Avram, “Give to me the people, and the possessions, take for yourself.”
21said And the king of Sodom unto Abram Give me the persons the goods and take
וַיֹּ֥אמֶר מֶֽלֶךְ סְדֹ֖ם אֶל אַבְרָ֑ם תֶּן לִ֣י הַנֶּ֔פֶשׁ וְהָֽרְכֻ֖שׁ קַֽח לָֽךְ׃
And Avram said to the king of Sodom, “I lift up my hand to Yahweh, El-Most High, Possessor of skies and land:
22said And Abram to the king of Sodom I have lift up mine hand unto the LORD God the most high the possessor of heaven and earth
וַיֹּ֥אמֶר אַבְרָ֖ם אֶל מֶ֣לֶךְ סְדֹ֑ם הֲרִמֹ֨תִי יָדִ֤י אֶל יְהוָה֙ אֵ֣ל עֶלְי֔וֹן קֹנֵ֖ה שָׁמַ֥יִם וָאָֽרֶץ׃
Not from a strap or sole of a sandal will I take from all that is yours, so that you can’t say, ‘I made Avram wealthy!’
23That I will not take from a thread even to a shoelatchet That I will not take any thing that is thine lest thou shouldest say I have made Abram
וְאִם מִחוּט֙ וְעַ֣ד שְׂרֽוֹךְ נַ֔עַל וְאִם אֶקַּ֖ח מִכָּל אֲשֶׁר לָ֑ךְ וְלֹ֣א תֹאמַ֔ר אֲנִ֖י הֶֽעֱשַׁ֥רְתִּי אֶת אַבְרָֽם׃
Only what the young men have eaten, and the portion of the men who went with me, Aner, Eshkol, and Mamre, they can take their portion.”
24Save only that which have eaten the young men and the portion only that which which went with me Aner Eshcol and Mamre let them take and the portion
