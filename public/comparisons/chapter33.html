Genesis 33

Chapter 33

1Jacob looked up and there was Esau, coming with his four hundred men; so he divided the children among Leah, Rachel and the two female servants.
1lifted up And Jacob his eyes and looked and behold Esau came and with him four hundred men And he divided the children unto Leah and unto Rachel and unto the two handmaids
וַיִּשָּׂ֨א יַֽעֲקֹ֜ב עֵינָ֗יו וַיַּרְא֙ וְהִנֵּ֣ה עֵשָׂ֣ו בָּ֔א וְעִמּ֕וֹ אַרְבַּ֥ע מֵא֖וֹת אִ֑ישׁ וַיַּ֣חַץ אֶת הַיְלָדִ֗ים עַל לֵאָה֙ וְעַל רָחֵ֔ל וְעַ֖ל שְׁתֵּ֥י הַשְּׁפָחֽוֹת׃
2He put the female servants and their children in front, Leah and her children next, and Rachel and Joseph in the rear.
2And he put the handmaids and her children foremost and Leah and her children after and Rachel and Joseph after
וַיָּ֧שֶׂם אֶת הַשְּׁפָח֛וֹת וְאֶת וִֽילָדֶ֙יהָ֙ רִֽאשֹׁנָ֑ה וְאֶת לֵאָ֤ה וִֽילָדֶ֙יהָ֙ אַֽחֲרֹנִֽים׃ וְאֶת רָחֵ֥ל וְאֶת יוֹסֵ֖ף אַֽחֲרֹנִֽים׃
3He himself went on ahead and bowed down to the ground seven times as he approached his brother.
3And he passed over before them and bowed himself to the ground seven times until he came near to his brother
וְה֖וּא עָבַ֣ר לִפְנֵיהֶ֑ם וַיִּשְׁתַּ֤חוּ אַ֙רְצָה֙ שֶׁ֣בַע פְּעָמִ֔ים עַד גִּשְׁתּ֖וֹ עַד אָחִֽיו׃
4But Esau ran to meet Jacob and embraced him; he threw his arms around his neck and kissed him. And they wept.
4ran And Esau to meet him and embraced him and fell on his neck and kissed him and they wept
וַיָּ֨רָץ עֵשָׂ֤ו לִקְרָאתוֹ֙ וַֽיְחַבְּקֵ֔הוּ וַיִּפֹּ֥ל עַל צַוָּארָ֖ו וַׄיִּׄשָּׁׄקֵ֑ׄהׄוּׄ וַיִּבְכּֽוּ׃
5Then Esau looked up and saw the women and children. “Who are these with you?” he asked. Jacob answered, “They are the children God has graciously given your servant.”
5And he lifted up his eyes and saw the women The children and said Who are those and said The children hath graciously given which God thy servant
וַיִּשָּׂ֣א אֶת עֵינָ֗יו וַיַּ֤רְא אֶת הַנָּשִׁים֙ וְאֶת הַיְלָדִ֕ים וַיֹּאמַ֕ר מִי אֵ֣לֶּה לָּ֑ךְ וַיֹּאמַ֕ר הַיְלָדִ֕ים אֲשֶׁר חָנַ֥ן אֱלֹהִ֖ים אֶת עַבְדֶּֽךָ׃
6Then the female servants and their children approached and bowed down.
6came near Then the handmaidens they and their children and they bowed themselves
וַתִּגַּ֧שְׁןָ הַשְּׁפָח֛וֹת הֵ֥נָּה וְיַלְדֵיהֶ֖ן וַתִּֽשְׁתַּחֲוֶֽיןָ׃
7Next, Leah and her children came and bowed down. Last of all came Joseph and Rachel, and they too bowed down.
7came And Leah also with her children and bowed themselves and after came Joseph and Rachel and bowed themselves
נִגַּ֥שׁ גַּם לֵאָ֛ה וִֽילָדֶ֖יהָ וַיִּֽשְׁתַּחֲוֽוּ׃ וְאַחַ֗ר נִגַּ֥שׁ יוֹסֵ֛ף וְרָחֵ֖ל וַיִּֽשְׁתַּחֲוֽוּ׃
8Esau asked, “What’s the meaning of all these flocks and herds I met?” “To find favor in your eyes, my lord,” he said.
8And he said What meanest thou by all this drove which I met And he said These are to find grace in the sight of my lord
וַיֹּ֕אמֶר מִ֥י לְךָ֛ כָּל הַמַּֽחֲנֶ֥ה הַזֶּ֖ה אֲשֶׁ֣ר פָּגָ֑שְׁתִּי וַיֹּ֕אמֶר לִמְצֹא חֵ֖ן בְּעֵינֵ֥י אֲדֹנִֽי׃
9But Esau said, “I already have plenty, my brother. Keep what you have for yourself.”
9said And Esau I have enough my brother
וַיֹּ֥אמֶר עֵשָׂ֖ו יֶשׁ לִ֣י רָ֑ב אָחִ֕י יְהִ֥י לְךָ֖ אֲשֶׁר לָֽךְ׃
10“No, please!” said Jacob. “If I have found favor in your eyes, accept this gift from me. For to see your face is like seeing the face of God, now that you have received me favorably.
10said And Jacob Nay I pray thee if now I have found grace in thy sight then receive my present at my hand as though I had seen the face as though I had seen the face of God and thou wast pleased with me
וַיֹּ֣אמֶר יַֽעֲקֹ֗ב אַל נָא֙ אִם נָ֨א מָצָ֤אתִי חֵן֙ בְּעֵינֶ֔יךָ וְלָֽקַחְתָּ֥ מִנְחָתִ֖י מִיָּדִ֑י כִּ֣י עַל כֵּ֞ן כִּרְאֹ֛ת פְּנֵ֥י כִּרְאֹ֛ת פְּנֵ֥י אֱלֹהִ֖ים וַתִּרְצֵֽנִי׃
11Please accept the present that was brought to you, for God has been gracious to me and I have all I need.” And because Jacob insisted, Esau accepted it.
11Take I pray thee my blessing that is brought hath dealt graciously with me to thee because God I have and because And he urged Take
וַיִּקָּֽח׃ נָ֤א אֶת בִּרְכָתִי֙ אֲשֶׁ֣ר הֻבָ֣את לָ֔ךְ כִּֽי חַנַּ֥נִי אֱלֹהִ֖ים וְכִ֣י יֶשׁ לִי כֹ֑ל וַיִּפְצַר בּ֖וֹ וַיִּקָּֽח׃
12Then Esau said, “Let us be on our way; I’ll accompany you.”
12And he said Let us take our journey
וַיֹּ֖אמֶר נִסְעָ֣ה וְנֵלֵ֑כָה וְאֵֽלְכָ֖ה לְנֶגְדֶּֽךָ׃
13But Jacob said to him, “My lord knows that the children are tender and that I must care for the ewes and cows that are nursing their young. If they are driven hard just one day, all the animals will die.
13And he said unto him My lord knoweth that the children are tender all the flock and herds with young are with me and if men should overdrive day them one will die all the flock
וַיֹּ֣אמֶר אֵלָ֗יו אֲדֹנִ֤י יֹדֵ֙עַ֙ כִּֽי הַיְלָדִ֣ים רַכִּ֔ים הַצֹּֽאן׃ וְהַבָּקָ֖ר עָל֣וֹת עָלָ֑י וּדְפָקוּם֙ י֣וֹם אֶחָ֔ד וָמֵ֖תוּ כָּל הַצֹּֽאן׃
14So let my lord go on ahead of his servant, while I move along slowly at the pace of the flocks and herds before me and the pace of the children, until I come to my lord in Seir.”
14I pray thee pass over Let my lord before his servant and I will lead on softly according as the cattle before according as and the children until I come Let my lord unto Seir
יַֽעֲבָר נָ֥א אֲדֹנִ֖י לְפָנַי֙ עַבְדּ֑וֹ וַֽאֲנִ֞י אֶֽתְנָהֲלָ֣ה לְאִטִּ֗י וּלְרֶ֣גֶל הַמְּלָאכָ֤ה אֲשֶׁר לְפָנַי֙ וּלְרֶ֣גֶל הַיְלָדִ֔ים עַ֛ד אֲשֶׁר אָבֹ֥א אֶל אֲדֹנִ֖י שֵׂעִֽירָה׃
15Esau said, “Then let me leave some of my men with you.” “But why do that?” Jacob asked. “Just let me find favor in the eyes of my lord.”
15said And Esau Let me now leave with thee some of the folk said What needeth it let me find grace in the sight of my lord
וַיֹּ֙אמֶר֙ עֵשָׂ֔ו אַצִּֽיגָה נָּ֣א עִמְּךָ֔ מִן הָעָ֖ם אֲשֶׁ֣ר אִתִּ֑י וַיֹּ֙אמֶר֙ לָ֣מָּה זֶּ֔ה אֶמְצָא חֵ֖ן בְּעֵינֵ֥י אֲדֹנִֽי׃
16So that day Esau started on his way back to Seir.
16returned that day So Esau on his way unto Seir
וַיָּשָׁב֩ בַּיּ֨וֹם הַה֥וּא עֵשָׂ֛ו לְדַרְכּ֖וֹ שֵׂעִֽירָה׃
17Jacob, however, went to Sukkoth, where he built a place for himself and made shelters for his livestock. That is why the place is called Sukkoth.
17And Jacob journeyed Succoth and built him an house for his cattle and made booths is called therefore the name of the place Succoth
וְיַֽעֲקֹב֙ נָסַ֣ע סֻכּֽוֹת׃ וַיִּ֥בֶן ל֖וֹ בָּ֑יִת וּלְמִקְנֵ֙הוּ֙ עָשָׂ֣ה סֻכֹּ֔ת עַל כֵּ֛ן קָרָ֥א שֵׁם הַמָּק֖וֹם סֻכּֽוֹת׃
18After Jacob came from Paddan Aram, he arrived safely at the city of Shechem in Canaan and camped within sight of the city.
18came And Jacob a city of Shechem which is in the land of Canaan came from Padanaram and pitched his tent before a city
בְּבֹא֖וֹ יַֽעֲקֹ֨ב שָׁלֵ֜ם הָעִֽיר׃ שְׁכֶ֗ם אֲשֶׁר֙ בְּאֶ֣רֶץ כְּנַ֔עַן בְּבֹא֖וֹ מִפַּדַּ֣ן אֲרָ֑ם וַיִּ֖חַן אֶת פְּנֵ֥י הָעִֽיר׃
19For a hundred pieces of silver, he bought from the sons of Hamor, the father of Shechem, the plot of ground where he pitched his tent.
19And he bought a parcel of a field where he had spread his tent at the hand of the children of Hamor father for an hundred pieces of money
וַיִּ֜קֶן אֶת חֶלְקַ֣ת הַשָּׂדֶ֗ה אֲשֶׁ֤ר נָֽטָה שָׁם֙ אָֽהֳל֔וֹ מִיַּ֥ד בְּנֵֽי חֲמ֖וֹר אֲבִ֣י שְׁכֶ֑ם בְּמֵאָ֖ה קְשִׂיטָֽה׃
20There he set up an altar and called it El Elohe Israel.
20And he erected there an altar and called it
וַיַּצֶּב שָׁ֖ם מִזְבֵּ֑חַ וַיִּ֨קְרָא ל֔וֹ אֵ֖ל אֱלֹהֵ֥י יִשְׂרָאֵֽל׃
